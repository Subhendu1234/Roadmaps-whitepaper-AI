## Key Tips for Success

1. **Consistency > Intensity**: Study 4-6 hours daily rather than cramming
2. **Build in Public**: Share your progress on LinkedIn/Twitter
3. **Network Actively**: Connect with 5 new AI professionals weekly
4. **Quality Projects**: Better to have 3 excellent projects than 10 mediocre ones
5. **Learn by Teaching**: Write blogs explaining concepts you learn
6. **Stay Updated**: Follow AI news (Papers with Code, Hugging Face)
7. **Health First**: Maintain work-life balance, exercise regularly
